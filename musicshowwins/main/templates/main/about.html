About Kpop Wins

Have you ever wondered which song by a kpop group got the most music show wins? Maybe you’re looking to see which kpop song achieved the most wins in a specific year. You’ve come to the right place!

You can view music show wins by a kpop artist or song at Top Wins. There you’ll find a list of the artists who’ve won the most, or check out the kpop songs with the most wins.

You can also use the Win Details page to view information on a group’s past victories. The site allows you to search for charts showing your chosen kpop group’s wins for each year and their top winning songs.

The site tracks the following Korean music shows:

  • • Music Bank
  • • Show Champion
  • • Music Core
  • • The Show
  • • MCountdown
  • • Inkigayo
